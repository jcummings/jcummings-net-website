Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in iOS app development, from concept to App Store launch. This includes native iOS apps, SwiftUI applications, AI/ML integration, and accessibility-focused solutions. I also offer consultation on app strategy and user experience design.

What's your development process like?

My process typically involves: initial consultation and requirements gathering, wireframing and prototyping, development with regular check-ins, testing and refinement, App Store submission, and post-launch support. I believe in transparency and regular communication throughout the project.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! I offer various support packages including bug fixes, iOS updates compatibility, performance optimization, and feature additions. I believe in building long-term relationships with clients to help their apps evolve and succeed.

How do you handle App Store submission?

I handle the entire App Store submission process, including optimizing app metadata, screenshots, and descriptions for better discoverability. I also assist with App Store Connect setup and can guide you through the review process.

Can you help with existing apps?

Absolutely! I can help with updating existing apps, adding new features, improving performance, fixing bugs, or modernizing older codebases to use the latest iOS technologies and design patterns.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

John Cummings ("I", "me", "my") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared across all my iOS applications and this website.

Information Collection

Data You Provide

  • Contact Information: When you contact me through this website or app support
  • App Data: Information you enter into my applications
  • Feedback: Reviews, ratings, and feedback you provide

Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage patterns (via Apple's App Analytics)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to fix bugs and improve performance
  • Website Analytics: Basic website usage statistics

How I Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve my applications and services
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Fix bugs and enhance app performance
  • Understand how my apps are used to guide future development
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers. This includes:

  • Personal tracking data in Total Life Tracker
  • Photos processed by FreshLook AI
  • Personal kindness logs in The Kindness Project
  • Automation configurations in Copilot Launchpad Pro

Cloud Storage

When you enable iCloud sync, data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account and encrypted using Apple's security standards. I do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

My apps may integrate with third-party services you choose to connect:

  • Apple Services: HealthKit, CloudKit, App Store
  • Optional Integrations: Various productivity services in Copilot Launchpad Pro

Your data shared with these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect my rights and safety
  • Anonymous, aggregated data for research purposes

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data I have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Portability: Export your data from my apps
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and data collection

Children's Privacy

My apps are not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact me immediately.

International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that information you provide may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from your jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates or website notifications. Your continued use of my apps constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact me:

  • Email: john@johncummings.net
  • Website: Contact Form

This privacy policy applies to all apps and services provided by John Cummings.

Copilot Launchpad Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026
Enterprise Security: Copilot Launchpad Pro handles business-critical data. We employ enterprise-grade security measures and transparency.

Information We Collect

Account and Profile Data

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Billing and subscription information
  • Team and workspace configurations
  • User preferences and settings

Automation Data

  • Workflow configurations: Automation rules, triggers, and actions
  • Service connections: OAuth tokens for third-party integrations
  • Execution logs: Automation run history and results
  • Custom scripts: JavaScript code you write for automations

Usage and Performance Data

  • Automation execution metrics and performance data
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs and diagnostic information
  • System performance and reliability metrics

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality

  • Execute automations: Run your workflows and integrations
  • Manage connections: Maintain secure links to third-party services
  • Provide analytics: Show productivity insights and automation performance
  • Enable collaboration: Support team workspaces and shared automations

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Identify and fix performance issues
  • Develop new integrations based on user needs
  • Enhance security and reliability

Third-Party Integrations

How Integrations Work

Copilot Launchpad Pro connects to services you authorize using industry-standard OAuth 2.0:

  • Secure authentication: We never see your passwords
  • Limited scope: Only access necessary permissions
  • Revokable access: Disconnect services anytime
  • Encrypted storage: All tokens encrypted at rest

Data Flow

When executing automations, data may flow between integrated services:

  • Data is processed according to your automation rules
  • Information is transmitted securely between services
  • We act as a secure bridge between your connected apps
  • Data processing is logged for auditing and debugging

Data Storage and Security

Encryption and Storage

  • Encryption at rest: All data encrypted in our databases
  • Encryption in transit: TLS 1.3 for all communications
  • Secure infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud security
  • Access controls: Role-based permissions and audit logs

Data Centers and Compliance

  • Data stored in SOC 2 certified facilities
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant data handling
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Enterprise customers can request on-premise deployment

Team and Enterprise Features

Team Workspaces

  • Shared automations: Team members can collaborate on workflows
  • Role-based access: Admins control who can view/edit automations
  • Audit logs: Track all changes and executions
  • Data isolation: Team data is isolated from other organizations

Enterprise Security

  • Single Sign-On (SSO) integration
  • Advanced user management and provisioning
  • Custom security policies and controls
  • Dedicated support and SLA agreements

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or automation data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help operate our service
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Safety and security: To protect users and prevent abuse

Your Privacy Rights

Data Access and Control

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your automations and data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct: Update or modify your information
  • Restrict: Limit how we process your data

Managing Integrations

  • View all connected services in your account settings
  • Revoke access to any third-party service instantly
  • Review and modify integration permissions
  • Monitor data flow and automation execution

Data Retention

Active Accounts

  • Automation data: Retained while your account is active
  • Execution logs: Kept for 90 days for debugging and analytics
  • Billing data: Retained as required by law and accounting needs

Account Deletion

  • Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Some data may be retained for legal and security purposes
  • Third-party service connections are immediately revoked

International Data Transfers

We may process data in various countries to provide our service. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard contractual clauses for data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Enterprise customers can specify data residency requirements

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect new features, legal requirements, or business changes. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to account holders
  • In-app notifications
  • Website announcements

Contact Us

Questions about privacy, security, or data handling:

  • Support: Priority Support
  • Enterprise: Contact Sales for enterprise-specific privacy questions

FreshLook AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026
Complete Privacy: All AI processing happens on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone.

Our Privacy Promise

FreshLook AI is built with privacy as the core principle. Unlike cloud-based photo editors, all AI enhancement happens directly on your iPhone using Apple's Core ML framework. This means:

  • Your photos never leave your device
  • No internet connection required for AI processing
  • No photo uploads to any servers
  • Complete control over your images

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your photos or images
  • Photo metadata or EXIF data
  • Processing history or edit details
  • Personal information from images
  • Location data from photos
  • Biometric data from processed images

Information We May Collect

Anonymous Usage Data (Optional)

With your permission, we may collect anonymous usage statistics:

  • Which AI models are used most frequently
  • General app usage patterns
  • Performance metrics (processing times, crashes)
  • Device compatibility information

This data cannot be linked to you personally and helps improve the app.

Support Communications

  • Information you provide when contacting support
  • App Store reviews and ratings
  • Feedback and feature requests

How AI Processing Works

On-Device Processing

  • Core ML Framework: Uses Apple's machine learning framework
  • Neural Engine: Leverages iPhone's dedicated AI hardware
  • Local Models: All AI models are downloaded and stored locally
  • No Cloud Dependency: Works completely offline

Photo Handling

  • Photos are processed in device memory only
  • Original photos are never modified
  • Enhanced versions are saved to your Photos app
  • No temporary copies sent anywhere outside your device

Data Storage

App Preferences

  • User interface settings (dark mode, etc.)
  • AI model preferences
  • Export quality settings

Stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud if enabled.

AI Models

  • Machine learning models downloaded from Apple's servers
  • Stored locally for offline processing
  • Updated periodically for improvements
  • No personal data included in models

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • iCloud: Optional sync of app preferences only
  • Core ML: On-device AI processing
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data (if enabled)

No Other Third Parties

FreshLook AI does not integrate with any other third-party services, social networks, or cloud photo services.

Your Privacy Controls

App Level Controls

  • Photo Access: Grant only when using the app
  • Analytics: Opt out of anonymous usage data
  • iCloud Sync: Disable preference syncing

iOS Level Controls

  • Photo Library Access: Control which photos the app can access
  • Analytics & Improvements: Disable in iOS Settings
  • App Permissions: Review and modify in Settings > Privacy

Data Security

  • Device Security: Protected by your iPhone's security features
  • No Network Transmission: Photos never sent over internet
  • Secure Element: AI models protected by iOS security
  • App Sandboxing: Isolated from other apps and data

International Users

Since all processing happens on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your photos remain in your device's secure environment regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated through app updates. Our commitment to on-device processing and photo privacy will not change.

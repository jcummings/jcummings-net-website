The Kindness Project Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026
Privacy First: Your personal kindness data stays on your device. We never share your individual acts of kindness.

Information We Collect

Personal Kindness Data (Stored Locally)

  • Acts of kindness you log
  • Personal reflections and notes
  • Your kindness streaks and achievements
  • Custom categories and preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync, which stores it securely in your personal iCloud account.

Anonymous Community Data

  • Total number of kind acts logged globally (no personal details)
  • General geographic regions for community challenges
  • Aggregate participation in community challenges

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

  • Display your personal kindness journey and statistics
  • Generate personalized kindness suggestions
  • Track your achievements and milestones
  • Sync across your devices (if you enable iCloud sync)

Anonymous Data Usage

  • Show global kindness impact statistics
  • Enable community challenges and leaderboards
  • Improve the app's kindness suggestions
  • Understand usage patterns for app improvements

Data Sharing and Community Features

What We Never Share

  • Your name or personal identity
  • Specific acts of kindness you've performed
  • Details about recipients of your kindness
  • Your exact location or personal information

Community Participation

When you participate in community challenges:

  • Your participation is anonymous
  • Only aggregate counts are shared (e.g., "50 people completed this challenge")
  • You can opt out of community features anytime
  • Geographic data is limited to general regions (city/state level)

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • Data encrypted on your device using iOS security standards
  • Protected by your device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • No server-side storage of personal data

iCloud Sync Security (Optional)

  • Encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Subject to Apple's security and privacy policies
  • Can be disabled anytime in app settings

Your Privacy Controls

  • Data Export: Export all your kindness data anytime
  • Community Opt-out: Disable community features and data sharing
  • iCloud Control: Enable/disable sync in Settings
  • Data Deletion: Delete all app data from device settings
  • Analytics Opt-out: Disable usage analytics in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

The Kindness Project is designed to be family-friendly. For users under 13:

  • No personal information is collected beyond what's stored locally
  • Community features can be disabled by parents
  • All data remains on the device unless iCloud sync is enabled

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect app improvements or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through app notifications.

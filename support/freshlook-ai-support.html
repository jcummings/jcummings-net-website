Frequently Asked Questions

AI processing speed depends on several factors:

  • Device Model: Newer iPhones with Neural Engine (iPhone X and later) process AI much faster
  • Image Size: Larger images take more time to process
  • Available Memory: Close other apps to free up RAM
  • Battery Level: iOS may throttle performance when battery is low

For best performance, use the app on iPhone 12 or newer with at least 50% battery.

For optimal AI enhancement results:

  • Choose the right model: Use Portrait mode for people, Landscape for scenic photos
  • Start with good lighting: AI works better with well-lit photos
  • Use high resolution: Process original quality images when possible
  • Adjust intensity: Use the sliders to fine-tune AI effects
  • Try before/after: Toggle to see the difference and adjust accordingly

FreshLook AI supports all standard iOS photo formats:

  • Input formats: HEIF, JPEG, PNG, RAW (on supported devices)
  • Output formats: HEIF (default), JPEG, PNG
  • Special formats: Live Photos (processed as still images)
  • Quality options: Choose from multiple quality levels for export

The app automatically handles format conversion while preserving maximum quality.

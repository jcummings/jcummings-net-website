Frequently Asked Questions

Logging acts of kindness is simple! Open the app and tap the "+" button on the main screen. You can either:

  • Select from suggested kindness prompts
  • Create a custom entry by tapping "Custom Act"
  • Use the quick-log widget from your home screen

Each entry can include details about what you did, who it helped, and how it made you feel.

Your privacy is our top priority. All personal information stays on your device. Only anonymized, aggregate data (like "10 acts of kindness were logged today globally") is shared to show community impact. We never share:

  • Your name or personal details
  • Specific acts of kindness you've performed
  • Location information beyond general city/region
  • Any identifiable information about recipients

Community challenges are collaborative goals that bring people together for positive impact. You can:

  • Join global challenges (like "1 Million Acts of Kindness Month")
  • Participate in local community challenges
  • Create private challenges for friends and family
  • Track progress together while maintaining individual privacy

Your participation is always anonymous, and you can leave challenges at any time.

  • Your location and local opportunities

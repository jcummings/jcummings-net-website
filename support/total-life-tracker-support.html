Total Life Tracker Support

Get help with tracking, analytics, and optimizing your life data

Frequently Asked Questions

Setting up tracking is easy! Start by:

  1. Tap the "+" button on the main dashboard
  2. Choose from pre-built templates (Health, Productivity, etc.) or create custom categories
  3. Configure data types (numbers, scales, yes/no, text entries)
  4. Set up reminders and tracking schedules
  5. Start logging your first entries!

The app includes guided setup to help you get started quickly.

Correlation analysis helps you discover connections between different aspects of your life:

  • Automatic detection: The app analyzes your data to find statistical correlations
  • Strength indicators: See how strong the relationship is between variables
  • Time-based analysis: Understand how changes in one area affect others over time
  • Insights generation: Get actionable insights based on your patterns

For example, you might discover that your sleep quality strongly correlates with your next-day productivity.

Total Life Tracker offers multiple export options:

  • CSV Export: Raw data for analysis in Excel or other tools
  • PDF Reports: Formatted reports with charts and insights
  • JSON Format: Complete data backup for developers
  • Chart Images: Export specific visualizations

Go to Settings → Data Export to access all export options. You own your data!

Yes! Total Life Tracker integrates with many iOS features and apps:

  • HealthKit: Automatic import of health and fitness data
  • Screen Time: Track digital wellness metrics
  • Calendar: Analyze schedule patterns
  • Weather: Correlate environmental factors
  • Shortcuts: Create custom automation flows

Enable integrations in Settings → Connected Services.

Goal setting and tracking is built into the app:

  1. Navigate to the Goals tab
  2. Create SMART goals (specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, time-bound)
  3. Connect goals to your tracking categories
  4. Set milestones and target dates
  5. Monitor progress with visual indicators

The app will show progress charts, send reminder notifications, and celebrate achievements!

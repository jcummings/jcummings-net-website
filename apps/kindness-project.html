Technical Excellence

Privacy First

Built with privacy as a core principle. All personal data stays on your device, with only anonymized kindness data shared to build global impact statistics.

Accessible Design

Full VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type compatibility, and high contrast mode ensure everyone can participate in spreading kindness.

Native iOS Experience

Built with SwiftUI and leveraging iOS features like Widgets, Shortcuts integration, and seamless synchronization across your devices via CloudKit.

Global Community

Connect with kindness advocates worldwide while maintaining complete anonymity. See global impact statistics without compromising individual privacy.
